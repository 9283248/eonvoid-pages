PRECISION CUTTING

How to Play

CutCraft is a precision geometry puzzle. Your goal is to slice shapes into perfectly equal parts.

  • Draw a cut: Swipe across the shape to create a cutting line
  • Aim for equal parts: Each piece shows its percentage — aim for evenly divided pieces
  • Limited cuts: You have a set number of cuts per level
  • Star rating: Earn up to 3 stars based on your precision accuracy
  • Undo & Reset: Made a bad cut? Use undo or reset to try again

Tips

  • Watch the accuracy gauge on the right — it shows your precision in real-time
  • The telemetry HUD displays coordinates and angles to help plan your cuts
  • Symmetrical shapes are easier to divide — use the grid lines as guides
  • Higher accuracy unlocks more stars and future levels
  • Some shapes need creative cut angles — don't just cut straight!

FAQ

Q: Does the game require internet?
A: No, CutCraft works completely offline.

Q: How many levels are there?
A: 50 levels across 2 worlds, with increasing complexity.

Q: How is accuracy calculated?
A: The game measures the area of each piece and compares them to the target equal split. Closer to equal = higher accuracy.

Q: Why can't I access some levels?
A: Levels unlock progressively. Earn at least 1 star on a level to unlock the next one.
