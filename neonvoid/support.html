How to Play

Neon Void is a fast-paced arcade shooter. Your ship fires automatically — your job is to survive.

  • Move: Touch anywhere and drag to move your ship
  • Shoot: Automatic — your ship fires continuously
  • Survive: Dodge enemies and destroy them for points
  • Bonus Life: Every 5,000 points you earn an extra life
  • Difficulty: Threat level increases every 7 seconds

Tips

  • Keep moving — standing still is a death sentence
  • Enemies come from all edges of the screen
  • Your ship has invulnerability frames after getting hit — use them wisely
  • Higher levels = more points per kill

FAQ

Q: Does the game require internet?
A: No, Neon Void works completely offline.

Q: How do I save my progress?
A: Your high score is saved automatically on your device.

Q: The game is too fast / too slow.
A: The game runs at 60fps. Make sure Low Power Mode is off for best performance.
