How to Use

Doc Lens turns your iPhone camera into a powerful portable document scanner.

  • Scan a document: Point your camera at a document — the app auto-detects the edges
  • Export as PDF: Save your scan as a high-quality PDF or JPG
  • OCR Text: Use the text recognition feature to extract editable text from your scan
  • E-Sign: Add your digital signature directly on any document
  • Organize: Create folders to keep your scans sorted and easy to find
  • Share: Send via Email, AirDrop, or any messaging app instantly

Tips

  • Ensure good lighting for the sharpest scans — avoid harsh shadows
  • Hold your phone steady and parallel to the document surface
  • Use the manual crop tool if auto-detection misses an edge
  • The batch scan mode lets you create multi-page PDFs in one session

FAQ

Q: Does the app require internet?
A: No, Doc Lens works completely offline. All processing happens on your device.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Absolutely. No scans or documents are ever uploaded to any server. Everything stays on your device.

Q: Which formats can I export to?
A: You can export scans as PDF (single or multi-page) and JPG image files.

Q: Does OCR support multiple languages?
A: Yes, OCR text recognition supports multiple languages using your device's built-in capabilities.
