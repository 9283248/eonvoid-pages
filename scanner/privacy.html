PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Doc Lens ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Doc Lens is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration.

3. Camera Access

The App requires access to your device's camera solely to scan documents. All image processing is performed locally on your device. No photos or scanned documents are uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

4. Locally Stored Data

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

• Scanned documents and PDF files you create
• User preferences and settings

This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

6. In-App Purchases

The App may offer in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any personal data from any users, the App is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
